Who we are

We believe thet no child should ever be bored. Playdate is a smart solution for the parents to find playdates for their kids and to meet up with other like minded parents. On Playdate, we show you other parents in your neighborhood with similar-aged children and create matches based on shared interests. Ideas

Kids need some fresh air!! Sometimes they just don’t know what to do outside, but here are some amazing, fun and easy ideas to keep them busy in the great outdoors!

Water baloon stomp

Break players into teams of two and give each team an equal number of water balloons. On your mark, players stomp the balloons, bursting them. The winning team is the one who pops all their balloons first.

Scavanger hunt

Print out the scavenger hunt directly onto a paper bag so the kids would have something to collect all of their goodies in this bag!!

Tea party

Host a lovely Tea Party in your garden with some healthy snacks and drinks to go along and let them have fun.

Art and Crafty smoke bombs

Have you ever made smoke bombs before? We have, and they are lots of fun. Let them try making colorful smoke bombs together by adding crushed sidewalk chalk to this simple playtime
